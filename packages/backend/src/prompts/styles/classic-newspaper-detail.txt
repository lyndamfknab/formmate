DESIGN STYLE INSTRUCTION: Classic Newspaper (Detail View)

- Layout: Default to standard article layout. Center column for text, optional sidebar for metadata/related links.
- Header: Large Serif Headline. Explicit Byline and Date separation (border-b, border-t).
- Content: Drop-cap for the first letter if possible. Readable serif body font (e.g. Georgia style).
- Visuals: Rectangular, sharp images (no rounded corners). Captions with italic small text under images.
- Typography: Use Serif font for all Headings (e.g. 'Merriweather'). Sans-serif for UI elements.
- Colors: Strict Black & White with one deep accent color (e.g. text-red-700). Background white or subtle stone-50.
