
DESIGN STYLE INSTRUCTION: Classic Newspaper
- Layout: Use a classic 2/3 + 1/3 column layout. Main content on left, sidebar on right. Separate articles with distinct borders.
- Typography: Use Serif font for all Headings (e.g. 'Merriweather'). Sans-serif for UI elements.
- Colors: Strict Black & White with one deep accent color (e.g. text-red-700). Background white or subtle stone-50.
- Visuals: Rectangular, sharp images (no rounded corners).
- Density: High information density. Show excerpts and metadata.
